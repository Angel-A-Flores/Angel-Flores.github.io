These are some of the animation and visual development work I assisted for a computer science group's senior project. The project was a video game under the title of Fragment and I assisted within the group's Art team. I assisted in the visual development aspects of the project, as well as creating animation assets for the game. The programs I used to create these works are Krita and FireAlpaca.
